## Full Description (paste in Play Console)
Scan Fit AI helps you track your weight faster and more consistently.

Use your camera with OCR to capture the scale value in seconds, edit it when needed, and follow your progress with goals and history.

### Key Features
- Camera capture with automatic OCR weight reading
- Manual correction for detected values
- Recent weight history
- Weight goal with progress bar
- Clean and fast daily flow

### Who it is for
- People focused on weight loss or weight management
- Users who want simple weekly tracking
- Anyone looking for a quick, low-friction routine

### Privacy
In this version, user data is stored locally on the device.

### Disclaimer
This app does not replace professional medical or nutrition advice.

Start today and track your progress with more consistency.
